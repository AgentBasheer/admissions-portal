FAQ: Your Specific Queries
Do I submit the Bank Certificate now or later? ‚ñº
Later. PDF Page 21, Item 6 says "Required only upon admission." The schedule on Page 5 also separates "Application Submission" and "Deadline for Proof of Financial Resources". You generally submit this document only after you have been successfully screened and admitted, usually to get your Certificate of Admission (CoA) for the visa.
Do I need to submit my SSC (Matric) Documents? ‚ñº
The PDF (Pg 8 & 21) only asks for High School Graduation (which corresponds to your HSSC/Intermediate). It does not explicitly request Middle School (SSC/Matric). However, carry your SSC documents just in case, but strictly speaking, only the HSSC Diploma (Apostilled) and Transcript are mandatory for the application.
Which documents need Apostille vs Notarization? ‚ñº
According to PDF Pg 21:
  • HSSC Diploma: Must be Apostilled.
  • HSSC Transcript: "Original or Notarized Original". (Though Apostille is recommended for Pakistan).
  • Family Relations Certificate: Notarized.
  • Pakistan Origin: No translation needed as your docs are in English (Pg 21).
