Frequently Asked Questions (CNU Admissions)
Is TOPIK Level 4 required for admission? ‚ñº
Yes, TOPIK Level 4 is required for Korean Track applicants. However, you can apply without TOPIK if you have an English certificate like IELTS 7.5+. Korean Track applicants without TOPIK Level 4 will be placed in a mandatory 36-credit Korean language program.
Can I apply without TOPIK using my IELTS score? ‚ñº
Yes, you can apply with IELTS 7.5+ score. English Track applicants can use either IELTS 7.5+ or TOPIK Level 4+ for admission. Your IELTS 7.5 score will be used for 50% of the admission evaluation.
What visa category will I get and can I transfer between campuses? ‚ñº
You will receive a D-2 visa (Degree Student). Campus transfer is possible after Year 1. Students typically complete the 36-credit Korean language program at Yeosu, then transfer to the main campus (Gwangju) for major studies. Business Administration majors study at Gwangju campus.
Is there an additional fee for the Korean language program? ‚ñº
No, there is no additional fee for the Korean language program. The regular tuition fees cover both the language program and subsequent major courses.
When do I need to submit the bank certificate? ‚ñº
The bank certificate showing KRW 16,000,000+ must be submitted with your initial application. It must be an original certificate (not statements) issued within 30 days of submission. All other documents must be received by the document submission deadline.
Will my 5-year study gap after high school be a problem? ‚ñº
The study gap will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. COVID-19 academic disruptions will be given contextual consideration. Your strong English proficiency (IELTS 7.5+) and high school scores (SSC 92% + HSSC 60%) will be considered favorably.
How should I fill out Form 3 for unusual circumstances? ‚ñº
For COVID-19 impacts, check "Cyber Learning/Online Classes". For post-graduation gap, select "Others" category. Explain family financial necessity honestly. Provide detailed, honest explanations for all unusual circumstances, clearly documenting COVID-19 impacts and family financial situations.
What are my chances of getting scholarships? ‚ñº
Semester 1 scholarships are not available (100% tuition payable). However, Semester 2 scholarships are unlocked based on your Semester 1 university grades. Target: Top 15% (GPA ~4.0+) for full tuition waiver (~1.9M KRW saved) or Top 30% (GPA ~3.5+) for half tuition waiver (~980k KRW saved).
